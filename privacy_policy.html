Privacy Policy for Junior Art Lab

Last Updated: January 2025

Junior Art Lab is a simple, kid-friendly drawing app designed for creative play. This privacy policy explains how the app handles data.

No Personal Data Collected

Junior Art Lab does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information from children or any user of the app.

Child Safety

  • The app is fully usable offline.
  • No login, account, or identifying information is required.
  • No drawing content is uploaded or stored on external servers.

Advertisements

The app displays Google AdMob child-safe, non-personalized ads. These ads comply with Google’s “Designed for Families” policy.

AdMob may collect basic device information solely for ad delivery (such as device model, crash logs, etc.) but no personal identifiers (age, name, location, etc.) are collected.

Parental Consent

A parent or guardian must accept the consent screen before the child uses the app. This screen explains ads, privacy, and app behavior.

Data Security

The app does not store any data online. Everything remains on the user’s device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Please review periodically.
